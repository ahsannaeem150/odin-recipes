Lasagna Recipe

Description

This Instructable will cover how to make lasagna the American way, and also how to make the meat sauce that goes in mine.

This lasagna recipe has changed a bit over the years but it's always been a favorite amongst my family and friends!

In the past couple years I've started using oven ready lasagna noodles that cook in the oven - I don't know that I'll ever go back to doing it the old way. :D

Ingredients

  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Butter and Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Make a layer of cooked lasagna noodles
  2. Spread an even layer of the ricotta cheese mixture.
  3. Spread an even layer of meat sauce.
  4. Repeat those layers two times.
  5. Top it with a final layer of noodles, sauce, mozzarella, and parmesan cheese.
  6. Spread a thin layer of pasta sauce in the bottom of a baking dish
